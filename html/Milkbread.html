Japanese Milk Bread

Soft and Fluffy Japanese Milk Bread!

Ingredients

  • Bread Flour: Bread flour has high protein content for more gluten development which is necessary for milk bread
  • Milk: 2% milk for this recipe but you can substitute non-fat or whole
  • Sugar
  • Active Dry Yeast: Yeast feeds on sugar to release carbon dioxide which proofs the bread
  • Dough Enhancer: Highly recommended for the softest,fluffiest of all milk breads in all the kingdoms. It also keeps bread fresh for days!
  • Salt
  • Butter

    • Instructions

    1. Add all the ingredients except the butter in a stand mixer
    2. once the dry flour is fully incorporated, add butter and mix on high
    3. Once you get a smooth, round ball in the mixer, check for proper gluten development. Can take 10-13 minutes; timing may vary
    4. Take a small piece of dough and stretch it between your fingers. If you can stretch it thin enough to see your fingerprint without it tearing, its raedy,
    5. Form the dough into a loose ball and place it in a proofing container greased with oil.
    6. Place in a warm, humid place away from sunlight until it doubles in size

    Shape And Form The Dough

    1. Divide the dough into 6 equal parts and form into a ball. Let it rest 15 minutes loosely covered.
    2. Roll out dough into an oval shape. Flip it over so the surface (crust) is on the bottom
    3. Letter Fold: Fold the top and bottom third over the center
    4. Rotate 90' and roll it up to form a spiral while pressing down
    5. Pinch seam closed and add three pieces seam side down into a loaf pan
    6. Proof in a warm, humid place until it doubles in size or just 1cm below the rim of the loaf pan

    Bake And Enjoy!

    1. Bake at 365' for 25 minutes or until there's a golden brown crust
    2. Immediately apply a thin layer of egg wash. Using pastry brush, brush on a thin later of the egg mixture. The heat from the bread will cook the egg, giving you a shiny golden crust
    3. Remove the loaves from the pan and allow to cool on a cooling rack. Enjoy atop of the Golden Throne before the Custodes obliterate you.
